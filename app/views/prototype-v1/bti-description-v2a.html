Please enter a detailed description of the goods

Specialty Garments

  • Provide the construction of the fabric (i.e. knit, woven, non-woven, etc.) and the generic name of each fiber and its weight.
  • Indicate how the garment is sold and marketed.
  • Provide the gender for which the garment is intended.
  • Indicate whether the fabric has been impregnated, coated, covered or laminated.
  • Specify whether the coating is cellular or compact.
  • Please provide the style number for each item.

Undergarments

  • Has the requestor stated that the garment is used for underwear?
  • Regarding brassieres, do they contain any lace, net or embroidery?”

Trade Agreements (Wearing Apparel and Textiles)

  • Identity the Trade Program or Agreement for which you are seeking a ruling.
  • For each component, provide the generic name of each fiber and its country of origin. Indicate whether the fiber is staple or filament. Also provide the weight for each generic fiber treating the staple and filament as separate materials.
  • State whether the goods will be shipped from one of the parties of the agreement directly to the United States.
  • Provide the country where the garment was produced (i.e. cut, sewn and assembled).
  • State where the pocket bag, sewing thread, narrow elastic fabric and/or lining was formed and finished.

Outerwear

  • Provide a sample (Due to coatings and fabric construction, a sample is imperative to classify outerwear).
  • Provide marketing and advertising to include hangtags and other labeling.
  • Provide fabric details of bonded fabric to include the type of fabric and fiber content by weight of each layer.
  • Please provide the style number for each item.

Hosiery

  • Regarding hosiery, has the manufacturer stated the yarn measurement in decitex?

Women’s Wearing Apparel

  • Provide a sample with the actual hangtags in size medium.
  • Submit the full fiber percentage by weight breakdown on the various parts of the garment/item.
  • State if the item number and whether it is knit, woven, non-woven, etc.
  • Provide marketing/advertising information.
  • Provide the gender for which the garment is intended.

Textile Fabrics

  • Provide two samples at least 18” x 18”.
  • What is the fiber content of each fabric layer, by percent of total weight?
  • Provide the total weight of the fabric (in grams per square meter), separately for each component or layer for bonded or coated fabrics.
  • Has the fabric been impregnated, coated, covered or laminated? If so, specify the material and weight in grams per square meter and by percentage.
  • Is the coating cellular or compact?
