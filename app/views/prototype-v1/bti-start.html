It is not legally required for your goods to have a Binding Tariff to import and export goods. You can find a code for your goods using the trade tariff step by step guidance and the Tariff lookup service. If you are not confident you have found the correct commodity code you can contact the Tariff Classification Service.

Use this service to:

  • do something
  • update your name, address or other details
  • do something else

The application will take about 15 minutes

Before you start

Before you apply for a BTI decision you may want to try finding the commodity code yourself using the following guidance and tools

  • Step by step guidance
  • Tariff lookup service
  • eBTI the EU database of existing eBTI rulings
  • Advice from the Tariff Classification Service

To apply for a BTI you will need:

  • A Government Gateway Account and registered for this service
  • Economic Operator Registration and Identification (EORI)number
  • Product details and supporting evidence
  • If you are an agent you will need a letter of representation fom your client
